About The Menu

Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)

Butter Chicken is a rich and creamy dish made with marinated chicken cooked in a spiced tomato-based sauce enriched with butter and cream. It's known for its smooth texture and aromatic flavors, often served with naan (Indian flatbread) or rice.

price:13$

Biryiani

Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish made with basmati rice, marinated meat (such as chicken, mutton, or fish), and a blend of spices. It’s often layered and cooked slowly, allowing the flavors to meld. Biryani is a celebratory dish commonly enjoyed at special occasions and gatherings.

price:20$

Palak paneer

Palak Paneer is a vegetarian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a smooth and spiced spinach puree. It’s healthy, vibrant in color, and full of flavor. This dish is often paired with naan or rice, making it a favorite in Indian households.

price:10$

about us


Welcome to Golden Paradise! At Spice Haven, we’re passionate about bringing the rich and diverse flavors of India to your plate. Established in 2005 by khaan puri, our restaurant is a celebration of authentic Indian cuisine, blending traditional recipes with contemporary flair. Our menu features a variety of dishes, from fragrant curries to mouthwatering tandoori items, all made with the freshest ingredients and a unique blend of spices. We take pride in sourcing locally and using organic ingredients whenever possible. Whether you’re here for a casual meal, a family gathering, or a special occasion, our warm and friendly staff are dedicated to providing an unforgettable dining experience. Join us at Spice Haven and embark on a culinary journey that will leave you craving more!
